    The Five Most Expensive Vape Mods on the Market Today

    May 17, 2022

    When most people start getting into vaping and discovering all the different devices there are to choose from, a common question comes up: what are the most expensive vape mods? It's natural to be curious about the prices of high end vapes on the market.

    Although some of these expensive vapes are in high demand and have interesting features and functionality to make up for the high costs, some of the vapes are one of a kind creations that make up for the price point. Which one would you spring for?

    The Five Most Expensive Vapes in the World

    The most expensive vape mods in the world come with some pretty hefty price tags. That’s because these vape mods come with interesting settings and features that most vapers would find interesting to check out (or try!).

    Expensive mods are becoming more and more in vogue as the popularity of vaping increases. Our expensive vape mods list covers creations from all over the globe. Check them out here.

    Gepetto Elite V2 Vape Mod — $2,500

    This is the fifth most expensive vape, checking it at the high price of $2,500. The Gepetto Elite V2 is a high end vape made from handcrafted wood.

    The Gepetto brand was the first company to mass market and produce custom vape mods, inspiring other vape manufacturers to follow suit. These high end mods are custom and completely handmade out of rare wood.

    The Elite V2 vape mod features an adjustable 510 with a brass connection pin. Another interesting feature of the device is the gold screw that helps adjust the easy access battery compartment.

    As a result of this dedication to craftsmanship and the exclusivity of this particular model, vapers across the world scramble to get one for themselves, resulting in massive bidding wars that drive up the price.

    If you're trying to get your hands on one of these vapes, be prepared to spend.

    Otto Carter Vape Mod — $3,000

    This vape mod is named after the skilled engraver himself, Otto Carter, although it might be less of a vape mod and more of a work of art given the skill set required to create it.

    If you'd like to purchase one for yourself, you're looking at an approximate cost of $3,000.

    Each Otto Carter vape mod is incredibly labour intensive, as he carves each piece by hand, guaranteeing that the final design is completely unique instead of mass produced.

    Although the engraver typically customises firearms, he also will produce custom vape mods upon special request. His work is breathtaking and often includes precious metals for the custom devices, hence the high price point.

    If you're in the market for a completely unique, high end vape mod for a vaper who's passionate about craftsmanship, this mod might be worth the money. It might even make it into a museum in the future.

    Top Hat Mods Vape Mod TI 26650 — $10,000

    Most vapers are familiar with the Top Hat brand. In fact, you can pick up most Top Hat mods vape products for an affordable price. However, this particular model is one of the most expensive vape mods around due to the gorgeous craftsmanship and special features. It has a titanium shell that is inlaid with wood, making it incredibly comfortable to hold and beautiful to look at.

    However, the most interesting feature of this vape mod is the SX350J battery which gives this vape incredible power.

    If you're looking to invest in one of these high end vape mods, be prepared to pay $10,000. This vape is super exclusive. It often sells out from the Top Hat online store, so you might have to snag yourself a model through a reseller.

    Magic Valley Vapors SX350J Dual Mod 18650 — $109,000

    Courtesy of Magic Valley Vapors, this expensive vape mod was created by a 3D printer and boasts a range of fascinating features.

    This 3D print was designed so that the user can change the battery without using any special tools. It is incredibly thin and lightweight but it can still hold two 18650 batteries.

    Designed in New York, this dual mod is crafted from platinum and costs a whopping $109,000. If that device seems a little far from your desired price range, you can always opt for the 14k gold version, which will only cost a comparably minimal $38,000.

    Sofia from Shisha Sticks — $887,000

    Clearly, money was no object when creating this custom vape mod. In all honesty, it seems as if the creator actually set out to create the most expensive vape around.

    The device was created for the girlfriend of a wealthy Russian billionaire for her birthday and is a one-of-a-kind custom mod, with only one ever produced. Hopefully she was happy to have received the most expensive vape mod around.

    This unique design from Shisha Sticks is encrusted with precious stones, including a six carat oval diamond at its tip that is reportedly worth around $60,000 on its own. In addition this expensive mod has a 24k gold button, 246 2k diamonds, and is made from hand-blown Italian Murano glass.

    How Much Should I Spend on a Vape?
